How to play

  • Left and right arrow keys to move back and forth.
  • Up arrow key to jump and the down arrow key to crouch.
  • Space bar to use Spark's special ability: transform into a fireball and roll to attack enemies.
  • While jumping, press the down arrow key to perform a ground pound and create an explosion.
  • Spark needs to score 40 points to win within 60 seconds, losing 5 points each time it gets hit.
  • Spark has 3 lives and loses one each time it takes damage.
  • Press "r" key to reset the game.

    • Good luck helping Spark complete its mission to protect humanity!
